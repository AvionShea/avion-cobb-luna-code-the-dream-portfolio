About Me

Hi everyone, my name is Avion. I am currently pursuing an Associate’s degree in Computer Science and plan to continue on to a Bachelor’s in Computer Science. My career goal is to become a software engineer and eventually move into a tech leadership position, where I can contribute to building tools that make a real impact.

Right now, I’m gaining hands-on experience with JavaScript, React, Node.js, and PostgreSQL, and I enjoy learning how to build projects that solve everyday problems. I also volunteer with organizations like Habitat for Humanity and Kramden Institute, which allows me to give back to the community while sharpening teamwork and problem-solving skills.

Outside of school and tech, I enjoy gaming, music, and riding my motorcycle. I’m excited to connect with classmates, collaborate on ideas, and grow together in this course. If you’d like to connect professionally, feel free to add me on LinkedIn.
